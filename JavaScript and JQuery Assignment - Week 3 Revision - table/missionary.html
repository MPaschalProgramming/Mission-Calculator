Bluer Ridge Climbing Missionary


About

Blue ridge climbing missionary is a mission I have started in order to bring the gospel of Christ to appalachian communities that have restricted access to traditional church establishments. At blue ridge climbing missionary we enable underserved communities to have an enjoyable day rock climbing in appalachia in an area that is accesible to them where hearing the Lord's merssage is all that is required to join in for an afternoon of fun climbing on the wall. Join us on Sundays at 1:00PM EST for an afternoon of giving back to our community through christ.
